1. Introduction

Welcome to Awaking Hero: RPG Training ("the App"), developed by kingtdes. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

By using Awaking Hero, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: email address and display name when you register via Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In).
  • Workout data: exercises, sets, reps, and training history you log in the app.
  • Game progress: XP, hero level, quests completed, and clan membership.
  • Health data (optional): if you grant permission, sleep duration/quality, heart rate, and step count read via Apple HealthKit or Android Health Connect. See Section 3 below — this data never leaves your device.
  • Device information: device type, operating system, IP address, and app version, collected via our crash-reporting provider Sentry. See Section 4 below.
  • Purchase information: which in-app gold packages you buy and whether the purchase succeeded, processed via Google Play and RevenueCat. We never see your payment card details.
  • AI Coach messages: if you use the AI Coach, your messages are sent to our server-side proxy to generate a response. See Section 5 below.
  • Notifications: if you opt in, we send push notifications about quests, challenges, and clan activity.

3. Health Data

Awaking Hero can optionally read sleep, heart rate, and step data from Apple HealthKit (iOS) or Health Connect (Android) to tailor your daily quests to your recovery — for example, suggesting a lighter quest after a poor night's sleep.

Health data is processed entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers, to Firebase, or to any third party, and is never used for advertising. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device's system settings (HealthKit or Health Connect permissions). If health data isn't available, the app may fall back to a step-count-based sleep estimate using your device's motion sensor — this estimate also stays on-device.

4. Crash Reporting (Sentry)

We use Sentry to automatically detect and diagnose crashes and errors. When an error occurs, a report may be sent that includes your device's IP address, device/OS information, and the app screen or action involved. This is used solely to improve app stability and is never used for advertising or profiling. Sentry is subject to Sentry's Privacy Policy. Crash reports are retained for a maximum of 90 days.

5. AI Coach

If you use the AI Coach feature, your messages are sent to our own server-side proxy, which forwards them to an AI language model provider to generate a response. We do not use these conversations for advertising or sell them to third parties. Your conversation history is stored encrypted on your own device, not centrally on our servers.

6. In-App Purchases

Gold purchases within the app are processed through Google Play's billing system via RevenueCat. We receive confirmation of which product was purchased, but never your payment card or billing details — those are handled directly by Google Play. RevenueCat is subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

7. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide and improve the core app experience (workouts, XP, leveling).
  • Sync your hero progress and workout history across devices.
  • Enable clan features and social leaderboards.
  • Tailor daily quests to your recovery, if you've enabled health data.
  • Send you relevant push notifications (only if you've enabled them).
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app stability.
  • Process in-app gold purchases.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

8. Firebase & Third-Party Services

Awaking Hero uses Google Firebase for authentication, database storage, and push notifications. Firebase is operated by Google LLC and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We may also use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app in aggregate. No personally identifiable information is shared with analytics providers beyond what Firebase collects by default. In addition to Firebase, we use Sentry (crash reporting) and RevenueCat (in-app purchases) as described in Sections 4 and 6.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Health data never reaches our systems and is retained only on your device, per its own OS settings. Crash reports are retained for a maximum of 90 days.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time via your device settings.
  • Revoke health data permissions at any time via your device settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: kingtdes@gmail.com

11. Children's Privacy

Awaking Hero is not intended for children under the age of 13. Players who indicate they are under 13 automatically enter Kids Mode, which disables clan/social features and all real-money purchases, and sends no data to Firebase. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is needed to enable Kids Mode. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

2. Informatie Die We Verzamelen

We kunnen de volgende soorten informatie verzamelen:

  • Accountgegevens: e-mailadres en weergavenaam wanneer je je registreert via Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In).
  • Workout data: oefeningen, sets, herhalingen en trainingsgeschiedenis die je in de app registreert.
  • Spelvoortgang: XP, held-level, voltooide quests en clanlidmaatschap.
  • Gezondheidsgegevens (optioneel): indien je toestemming geeft, slaapduur/-kwaliteit, hartslag en stappen, gelezen via Apple HealthKit of Android Health Connect. Zie sectie 3 hieronder — deze gegevens verlaten nooit je apparaat.
  • Apparaatinformatie: apparaattype, besturingssysteem, IP-adres en app-versie, verzameld via onze crashrapportage-provider Sentry. Zie sectie 4 hieronder.
  • Aankoopinformatie: welke in-app goud-pakketten je koopt en of de aankoop is gelukt, verwerkt via Google Play en RevenueCat. We zien nooit je betaalgegevens.
  • AI Coach-berichten: als je de AI Coach gebruikt, worden je berichten naar onze server-side proxy gestuurd om een antwoord te genereren. Zie sectie 5 hieronder.
  • Meldingen: als je je aanmeldt, sturen we pushmeldingen over quests, uitdagingen en clanactiviteit.

3. Gezondheidsgegevens

Awaking Hero kan optioneel slaap-, hartslag- en stappengegevens lezen via Apple HealthKit (iOS) of Health Connect (Android) om je dagelijkse quests af te stemmen op je herstel — bijvoorbeeld een lichtere quest voorstellen na een slechte nachtrust.

Gezondheidsgegevens worden volledig op je eigen apparaat verwerkt. Ze worden nooit naar onze servers, naar Firebase, of naar derden verzonden, en nooit gebruikt voor advertenties. Je kunt deze toestemming op elk moment intrekken via de systeeminstellingen van je apparaat (HealthKit- of Health Connect-toestemmingen). Is er geen gezondheidsdata beschikbaar, dan kan de app terugvallen op een schatting op basis van je bewegingssensor (stappenteller) — ook die schatting blijft alleen op je apparaat.

4. Crashrapportage (Sentry)

We gebruiken Sentry om crashes en fouten automatisch te herkennen en te diagnosticeren. Bij een fout kan een rapport worden verzonden met daarin het IP-adres van je apparaat, apparaat-/OS-informatie, en het schermonderdeel of de actie waar de fout plaatsvond. Dit wordt uitsluitend gebruikt om de stabiliteit van de app te verbeteren en nooit voor advertenties of profilering. Sentry valt onder het privacybeleid van Sentry. Crashrapporten worden na maximaal 90 dagen verwijderd.

5. AI Coach

Als je de AI Coach-functie gebruikt, worden je berichten verzonden naar onze eigen server-side proxy, die ze doorstuurt naar een AI-taalmodel-provider om een antwoord te genereren. We gebruiken deze gesprekken niet voor advertenties en verkopen ze niet aan derden. Je gespreksgeschiedenis wordt versleuteld op je eigen apparaat bewaard, niet centraal op onze servers.

6. In-App Aankopen

Goud-aankopen binnen de app worden verwerkt via Google Play's betaalsysteem, via RevenueCat. Wij ontvangen een bevestiging van welk product is gekocht, maar nooit je betaal- of kaartgegevens — die worden rechtstreeks door Google Play verwerkt. RevenueCat valt onder het privacybeleid van RevenueCat.
