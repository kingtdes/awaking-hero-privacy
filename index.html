1. Introduction

Welcome to Awaking Hero: RPG Training ("the App"), developed by kingtdes. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

By using Awaking Hero, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: email address and display name when you register via Firebase Authentication.
  • Workout data: exercises, sets, reps, and training history you log in the app.
  • Game progress: XP, hero level, quests completed, and clan membership.
  • Device information: device type, operating system, and app version for crash reporting and analytics.
  • Notifications: if you opt in, we send push notifications about quests, challenges, and clan activity.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide and improve the core app experience (workouts, XP, leveling).
  • Sync your hero progress and workout history across devices.
  • Enable clan features and social leaderboards.
  • Send you relevant push notifications (only if you've enabled them).
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app stability.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Firebase & Third-Party Services

Awaking Hero uses Google Firebase for authentication, database storage, and push notifications. Firebase is operated by Google LLC and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We may also use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app in aggregate. No personally identifiable information is shared with analytics providers beyond what Firebase collects by default.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time via your device settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: kingtdes@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

Awaking Hero is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Welkom bij Awaking Hero: RPG Training ("de App"), ontwikkeld door kingtdes. Dit Privacybeleid legt uit hoe wij je persoonlijke gegevens verzamelen, gebruiken en beschermen wanneer je onze app gebruikt.

2. Informatie Die We Verzamelen

We kunnen de volgende soorten informatie verzamelen:

  • Accountgegevens: e-mailadres en weergavenaam wanneer je je registreert via Firebase Authentication.
  • Workout data: oefeningen, sets, herhalingen en trainingsgeschiedenis die je in de app registreert.
  • Spelvoortgang: XP, held-level, voltooide quests en clanlidmaatschap.
  • Apparaatinformatie: apparaattype, besturingssysteem en app-versie voor crashrapportage en analyses.
  • Meldingen: als je je aanmeldt, sturen we pushmeldingen over quests, uitdagingen en clanactiviteit.

3. Hoe We Je Informatie Gebruiken

Je gegevens worden gebruikt om:

  • De kern app-ervaring te leveren en te verbeteren (workouts, XP, levelen).

Wij verkopen je persoonlijke gegevens niet aan derden.

4. Firebase & Diensten van Derden

Awaking Hero gebruikt Google Firebase voor authenticatie, database-opslag en pushmeldingen. Firebase wordt beheerd door Google LLC en valt onder het privacybeleid van Google.
